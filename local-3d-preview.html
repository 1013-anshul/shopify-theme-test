Old things,
written down
carefully.

We started Vura because the things our grandmothers kept in tins — phitkari, the crystalline mineral salt — worked, and nothing on the shelf admitted to using them.

The first formulation begins as an entry in the Mineral Archive: a single mineral, a documented intent, no more than three botanicals. The entry is dated. The lot is logged. The bottle carries the lot.

The archive is open. If you want to know what's in lot 24·17·002 — the ratio of phitkari to rose hydrosol, the pH after 48 hours, the supplier of the alum — write to us. We'll tell you. That's the whole point.

Frequently Asked Questions

Our Phitkari block spritz is a natural skin mist composed of pressed potassium alum crystal mineral, distilled Kannauj copper-still rosewater, and wild cold-pressed aloe vera. It acts as an organic, chemical-free astringent that tightens pores, calms irritation, and protects the skin barrier.
We recommend using it twice daily—immediately after cleansing your face in the morning, and at night before bed. Spray 2-3 pumps directly onto clean face or spray on your hands and pat into the skin.
To maintain absolute mineral potency and freshness, we craft Vura in small runs of exactly 200 bottles. Each bottle is hand-labeled and printed with a unique batch registry number (e.g. Series 01 / Lot No. 24·17·002) indicating its origin.
