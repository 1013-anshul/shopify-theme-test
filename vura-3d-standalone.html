Potassium Alum KAl(SO₄)₂·12H₂O

Raw crystal mineral blocks sourced from natural geothermal deposits. Pressed and shaped by hand. Acts as a powerful natural astringent that tightens pores, calms irritation, and seals skin post-wash.

Rosa Damascena Kannauj Distillate

Damask rose petals hand-harvested at dawn and steam-distilled in traditional copper vessels. This ancient copper-still hydrosol cools, tones, and infuses the formula with active, hydrating properties.

Aloe Barbadensis Cold-Pressed Gel

Succulent leaves cold-pressed within hours of harvest to retain polysaccharides. Delivers instant skin cell hydration, forms a breathable moisture barrier, and accelerates healing of micro-abrasions.
