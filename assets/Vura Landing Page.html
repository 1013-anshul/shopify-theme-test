Old things,
written down
carefully.

We started Vura because the things our grandmothers kept in tins — phitkari, the crystalline mineral salt — worked, and nothing on the shelf admitted to using them.

The first formulation begins as an entry in the Mineral Archive: a single mineral, a documented intent, no more than three botanicals. The entry is dated. The lot is logged. The bottle carries the lot.

The archive is open. If you want to know what's in lot 24·17·002 — the ratio of phitkari to rose hydrosol, the pH after 48 hours, the supplier of the alum — write to us. We'll tell you. That's the whole point.

— Aanya & Vikram, founders
